Overview

Water Tracker Lite ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to any of your data, and it is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data. There are no third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools integrated into the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by all age groups.
